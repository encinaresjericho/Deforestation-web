About us

Welcome to our platform, we are passionate group of IT students come together to address the urgent issue of deforestation. Our journey began with a shared concern for the rapid decline of forested areas and the profound effects this has on our planet. Witnessing the devastating impact firsthand fueled our desire to take action. We believe that knowledge is power, and by leveraging our technical skills and dedication, we can make a positive impact. Through this website, we aim to raise awareness about deforestation and promote sustainable solutions. Join us as we work towards combating deforestation and protecting our planet for future generations.
